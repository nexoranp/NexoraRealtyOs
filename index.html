FAQ

Common Questions Answered

Is Nexora RealtyOS just a website?
+

No. Nexora RealtyOS is a complete real-estate business operating system. Beyond the website, it includes a property listing management system, buyer lead CRM, site visit tracking, follow-up reminders, agent dashboard, social media management, property video organization, and dashboard analytics — all from one platform.

Can we choose our own website design?
+

Yes. The Basic plan includes professionally designed ready-made templates — just choose one and set up fast. The Plus and Pro plans include custom website design options where you can share your own design idea, reference website, preferred colors, layout, or branding style. Nexora can also create a custom website design based on your agency's brand if you prefer.

How fast can we get started?
+

With a ready-made template, your agency website and dashboard can be set up in around 30 minutes after the required agency details and listings are provided. For custom website design (Plus and Pro plans), the timeline depends on the complexity of the design, but the process starts immediately after your plan is confirmed.

Can buyers contact us through WhatsApp or Viber?
+

Yes. Every Nexora RealtyOS website includes one-click WhatsApp, Viber, and call action buttons so buyers can contact your agency instantly through their preferred channel. All inquiries are also captured through inquiry forms that feed directly into your lead dashboard.

Does the system help manage leads and follow-ups?
+

Yes. Leads are automatically captured in your dashboard with buyer name, phone, interested property, source, status, budget, and notes. You can assign leads to agents, set follow-up reminders, track lead status, schedule site visits, and move buyers through the sales process from one place.

Do you help with social media and property videos?
+

Yes. Social media handling and content organization are included in Plus and Pro plans. The Pro plan also includes professional property video and reel creation support from a reputed real-estate content creator or media partner — giving your listings a more professional visual presence.

Is there custom pricing for larger agencies?
+

Yes. Custom pricing is available for large agencies, land developers, housing projects, and businesses that need advanced workflows, custom project pages, dedicated campaign support, or specialized system configurations. Contact Nexora directly to discuss your specific needs.
